PSA Population Report

PSA maintains the current certified population record for the 2018 Topps Future Stars FS-5 Shohei Ohtani Black.

Population totals can change when additional cards are graded, certifications are corrected or database records are updated. For that reason, this archive does not treat a copied population number as permanently current.

How the archive labels claims

Documented fact
Supported by a source that readers can inspect.
Inference
A conclusion drawn from documented facts, clearly identified as an inference.
Collector terminology
Language used by some members of the hobby, not an official manufacturer or grading designation.
Interpretation
A proposed meaning or significance presented for readers to evaluate.
