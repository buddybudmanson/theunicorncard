Articles and Resources

Published writing, historical references, research materials and external resources relating to The Unicorn Card.

Purpose

The Unicorn Card Archive exists to document the history, recognition, identification and provenance of the 2018 Topps Future Stars FS-5 Shohei Ohtani Black Parallel, known within the collecting community as The Unicorn Card.

This page gathers published resources that contribute to that historical record.

Published References

As additional books, articles, interviews, podcasts and historical references are published, they will be added here to preserve a documented record of the card's history.

Where possible, references will distinguish between:

  • Documented historical fact
  • Collector terminology
  • Interpretation
  • Editorial commentary

Editorial Policy

The Unicorn Card Archive strives to separate documented evidence from collector interpretation. Historical claims are presented as factual only when supported by publicly available evidence or reliable documentation.
