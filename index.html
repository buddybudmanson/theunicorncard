The sequence

Obscurity. Misidentification. Recognition.

01

Obscurity

The card carried no serial number announcing what it was. Its rarity could remain unseen.

02

Misidentification

Some examples entered slabs under labels that failed to identify the Black parallel.

03

Recognition

The collecting community began reconstructing the card's identity, population and historical significance.

The surviving evidence

The mistake survived.

A mislabel remains a mistake. But a Unicorn Card still sealed inside a mislabeled holder preserves something more than an error: physical evidence that the card once passed through the modern hobby under the wrong identity.

“The mislabel is not the metaphor. The mislabel is the surviving evidence that the metaphor happened.”
