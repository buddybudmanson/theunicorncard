Definitions

The core terms used throughout The Unicorn Card Archive.

The Unicorn Card

The Unicorn Card

The Unicorn Card is the 2018 Topps Future Stars FS-5 Shohei Ohtani Black Parallel.

True Unicorn

The “True Unicorn” Card

The “True Unicorn” card is the 2018 Topps Future Stars FS-5 Shohei Ohtani Black Parallel still encapsulated in a mislabeled holder.

The phrase is collector terminology rather than an official Topps or grading-company designation.

Mislabel

Mislabel

A mislabel is a grading label that does not correctly identify the card inside the holder.

Provenance

Provenance

Provenance is the documented history of an object.

Recognition

Recognition

Recognition is the process by which an object becomes correctly identified and understood.

Stealth Transaction

Stealth Transaction

A Stealth Transaction is a sale involving a Unicorn Card that is not accurately captured under the Black parallel because the card was catalogued under another identity.
