Why identification matters

When a card is catalogued under the wrong version, the consequences extend beyond one label. Population reports, sales databases and market histories may classify the transaction under the wrong item. The object exists, but its record can remain hidden from searches for the Black parallel.

This archive refers to that condition as stealth mode: not a technical grading term, but a descriptive phrase for a card whose identity may remain obscured within modern cataloguing systems.
